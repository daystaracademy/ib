Freeing Knowledge

Teachers' goals should be helping students to achieve high marks. And to do so they should not keep the resources to success from students. In light of the fact that the economics teacher Mr. Darcy refuses to share his official course PPTs with students, many students faced huge obstacles and great difficulties in preparing for IB economics exams. For this reason, we built this revision hub with sincere dedication. We firmly believe that educational resources and academic knowledge should never be restricted or withheld. Our core ambition is to break down all barriers to learning, gather complete and practical review materials openly, and make high-quality educational knowledge truly accessible and freely available to every learner without exception. Success is certainly attainable!
